# Joystick---LED-LCD-SP-
(SP) = Small Project, Indicating a minor project, which therefore is just beginner level and should not be flexed or used in portfolios.


Hardware used:

1 × Arduino
1 × Breadboard
1 × I2C LCD
1 × Joystick module
4 × LEDs (Red, Yellow, Blue, Green)
4 × Current-limiting resistors
9 × Female-to-Male jumper wires
12 × Male-to-Male jumper wires
